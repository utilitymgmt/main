Who We Are

Through Utility Management Group, you have the power, not your utility company

Utility Management Group helps businesses take back control of the costs of their electric and gas bills. If your business is situated in a deregulated energy market, your utility company no longer handles the supplier portion of the bill. What this means is that if a consumer does not choose a dedicated supplier for their utility bill, they are left on what's called the default service rate, aka price-to-compare rate, which is subject to great market fluctuations and can be highly unstable for a business to properly manage their finances. UMG helps businesses be strategic in handling their energy supply by providing access to the most reputable suppliers in the country, and negociating rates on your behalf. These are the same suppliers with whom entire cities have contracted for their residents, no gimicks, just straight forward and long term stability.
