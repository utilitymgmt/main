Welcome to Utility Management Group

You have the power, not your utility company

If your business or home is situated in a deregulated energy market, your electric and gas supply now works independently of your utility company. What this means is that if a consumer does not choose a dedicated supplier for their utility bill, they are left on what's called the utilty default rate, aka price-to-compare rate. This rate is subject to massive market fluctuations and can expose a business to great instabilty in some businesses's and homes's largest expenses. 

Utility Management Group helps businesses be strategic in handling their energy supply by providing access to the most reputable suppliers in the country and negotiating rates on your behalf.  Don't leave it up to chance; talk with one of our energy advisors to set things right.
